FAQ

What is Rule of Thumb?
This is a game that measures the time it takes for you to click a sequence of blinking squares. The faster, the better. The better you play, the harder it gets.
Can I play against friends?
Yes. Issue challenges to friends. If you are better than them, you gain levels, and they lose. The higher your level is, the harder the game becomes.
Is Rule of Thumb free to play?
Yes. You can increase the number of challenges you can send by purchasing packs of challenges.
Is the game fair even if you don't pay?
Yes. The only thing you change by paying is the number of challenges you can send to friends. Game mechanics do not change!
